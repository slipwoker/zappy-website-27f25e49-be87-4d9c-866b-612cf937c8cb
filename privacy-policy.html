1. Introduction

Deal360 ("we", "our", "the company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Deal360 system.

Contact Information:

  • Company Name: Deal360
  • Address: 39 Jerusalem Street, Safed, Israel
  • Email: deal360.crm@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Contact details: Name, phone, email address
  • Company details: Company name, role, professional licenses
  • Account data: Username, encrypted password
  • Client details (name, phone, ID, preferences)
  • Communication history and meetings
  • Property requirements and preferences
  • Call recordings (with explicit consent)
  • Automatic call transcripts
  • Analysis of call content for service improvement
  • Property details (address, price, description)
  • Property photos and documents
  • Transaction history
  • IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Usage data of the system
  • Activity logs for security purposes

3. How We Use Information

3.1 Purposes of Use

  • Account management and system access
  • Smart matching between clients and properties
  • Transaction management and digital signatures
  • Service improvement through data analysis
  • Technical support and troubleshooting
  • We use advanced algorithms to analyze your preferences
  • Automatic matching between clients and properties
  • Call transcript analysis for identifying opportunities
  • We allow login with Google for easier authentication
  • We only use your basic Google profile information (name, email)
  • We do not access, store, or share any other Google data

4. Data Security

4.1 Technical Protections

  • Data encryption: 256-bit SSL encryption
  • Secure tokens for every connection
  • Encrypted storage in secure databases
  • Encrypted backups in multiple locations
  • Restricted access to authorized staff only
  • Regular security training for team members
  • Periodic external security audits
  • Data is retained as long as the account is active
  • Data is deleted within 30 days of account closure
  • Security backups are deleted within 90 days

5. Information Sharing with Third Parties

5.1 When We Share

  • Technical service providers (servers, cloud storage)
  • Payment services for processing charges
  • Legal obligations in case of lawful requests
  • We never sell your personal information
  • We do not share data for advertising or external marketing
  • Your client data remains strictly confidential

6. Your Rights

6.1 Right of Access

  • Receive a copy of your personal information
  • Know how your data is being used
  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Add missing information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Request deletion of specific data (when possible)
  • Receive your data in a commonly used format
  • Transfer your data to another service provider
  • Object to certain data processing
  • Withdraw consent given previously

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

7.1 Types of Cookies

  • Essential cookies: For basic system functionality
  • Performance cookies: To improve the system
  • Analytics cookies: To understand usage patterns
  • You can manage cookie preferences in your account settings
  • Some cookies are necessary for system functionality

8. Compliance

8.1 Israeli Regulations

  • Privacy Protection Law, 1981
  • Basic Law: Human Dignity and Liberty
  • Privacy Protection Regulations (Data Security), 2017
  • If you are located in the EU, extended rights apply
  • Right to erasure and data portability
  • Breach notifications within 72 hours

9. Minors

This system is intended for adults only. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under the age of 18. If we discover such data was collected, it will be deleted immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

10.1 Updates

  • We will notify you of significant changes via email
  • The updated policy will be published on our website
  • Continued use of the system constitutes acceptance of changes
  • Previous versions are available upon request
  • Changes take effect 30 days after notification
